Magic

Medium: Collage   Tools: Magazine Clippings, Printed Photographs

Marbles, symbolic of wonder and mystery to me, bring a light feel to the composition, resembling bubbles suspended in time while rich ochres and golden hues evoke a sense of nostalgia.

This piece is a reminder that the process of maturing is a dance between the comforting familiarity of the past and the uncertain future that awaits.
