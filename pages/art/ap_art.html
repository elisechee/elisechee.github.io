Notes on Love in Music & Cinema

Medium: Digital Art   Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Procreate

This collection investigates the storytelling power of film, music, and art when combined. As I ventured into this process, I chose to portray the musical and visual narratives that reflected the personal connections of my own life. With each piece, I hoped to give a glimpse into how I absorb the world around me thorugh film and music and process it thorugh visual art
