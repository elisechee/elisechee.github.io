"Design" Accordian Poem Book

Medium: Print   Tools: Procreate, Adobe Photoshop

In my accordion-style poetry book, I sought to depict the playful and deeply thoughtful free verse of Billy Collins' poem, "Design." Through diverse colors and shapes, my intention is to engage the reader in a multi-sensory experience, mirroring the poem's exploration of the complexities inherent in seemingly ordinary moments.
